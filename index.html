Hitoshi Shinso

About Shinso

Hitoshi Shinso is a character from Boku no Hero Academia (also know as My Hero Academia). He has a desire to be a hero despite his quirk being seen as villianous. Due to the nature of his quirk he ended up in the Gen Ed class, but because to his hardwork and dedication he proved himself and has been moved to the hero course.

Shinso and his hero costume

Characterisitics

  1. His quirk is brainwash. He can control anyone who answers him.
  2. He has worked with Aizawa to learn how to use a capture device
  3. He has a support item that can change the sound of his voice

Shinso's Friends

Izuku Midoriya first meets Shinso in the U.A. sports festivial and they delevoped a camaraderie after their battle. Denki Kaminari befriended Shinso despite his cold demeanor in a Joint Training Battle which served as a test for Shinso elgibility into the hero course. Neito Monoma took a liking to Shinso after he insulted Class 1-A, or more specifically Katsuki Bakugo.
